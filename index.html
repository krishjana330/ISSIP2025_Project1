Welcome to ISSIP 2025

The 2025 Fifth International Symposium on Signal and Image Processing (ISSIP 2025) is aimed to foster the field of signal and image processing encompassing the theory and practice of algorithms and hardware that convert signals produced by artificial or natural means into a form useful for specific purpose.

The signals might be speech, audio, images, video, sensor data, telemetry, electrocardiograms, or seismic data among others. The possible application areas include transmission, display, storage, interpretation, classification, segmentation, or diagnosis.

The Rochester Institute of Technology Kosovo (A.U.K), Kosovo, Europe, organized the symposium on November 24-25, 2025. The main objective of the symposium is to provide a platform for young researchers to showcase their inventions and innovations in the field of signal and image processing which may be used by mankind.

ISSIP 2025 is technically sponsored by the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society, Kolkata Chapter, IEEE CIS Kolkata Chapter, and the Asia-Pacific Artificial Intelligence Association (AAIA), Kolkata Branch.

Call for Papers

Topics of interest will fall under one or more of the symposium tracks.

Digital Signal Processing

  • Robust and low complexity filter design
  • Signal reconstruction
  • Filter bank theory and wavelets

Statistical Signal Processing

  • Adaptive filtering
  • Learning algorithms for neural networks
  • Hybrid soft computing techniques
  • Spectrum estimation and modeling
  • Sensor array processing with applications in sonar and radar

Image Processing

  • Image restoration
  • Image compression
  • Image segmentation
  • Image quality evaluation
  • Computer vision and medical imaging
  • Character recognition
  • Remote Sensing Imagery

Speech Processing

  • Modeling
  • Compression
  • Speech recognition

Video Processing

  • Video compression
  • Analysis and processing
  • 3D video compression
  • Automated and distributed crowd analytics
  • Stereo-to-auto stereoscopic 3D video conversion
  • Virtual and augmented reality

Data Processing and Analysis

  • IoT and Big data analysis
  • Portfolio data analysis
  • Blockchain applications to data analysis
  • Data visualization
  • Optimization strategies in engineering, business and health
  • Digital/HR based data analysis management
  • Infrastructure and data centers
